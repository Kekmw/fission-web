About Us

Welcome to Fission Entertainment! We are a game development business and this is our hub for all our games, everyone is welcome here even if you don't play our games, thank you for being a part of the community!

Why Us?

  • We want to make games that the community would really enjoy, we listen and appreciate any feedback from you, you can help decide any functions in our games, Inferno, or even our servers!
  • Our goals is not focused on only profits, our main goal is to make great games, we truely want you to enjoy the experience of any game in Fission.
  • We aim to go above and beyond for our community, we like perfecting our content, @Inferno our specialized bot, who has many fun and useful features for our team, is an example you can use to determine what to expect from us!

Our team combines passion and expertise to deliver high-quality games that captivate players.

Our Mission

Some game development businesses focus more on the profits rather than the experience of the players, others established an unstable workplace culture for staff, we want to be a place where developers can feel very happy, we want this to be an amazing place to work at, we also want the player to truely feel like their voice matters, with every suggestion sparking quick action, and adding features and functions only for the experience of the player even if it would hurt profits, we want to ensure the best experience for the team and community of Fission, we aim to be the best game development business for any player or staff, and we are highly commited towards making that happen.


If you ever feel like we did a very good job or impacted you really positively and you want to help us on our journey, you can donate (coming soon), donations are greatly appreciated.

Thank you for your time!
